What is FlowState?

FlowState is a mood-driven productivity platform that adapts to your emotional state, helping you work smarter and feel better throughout your day.

Mood-Based Discovery

Select your current emotional state and receive curated music, movies, and games tailored to enhance your productivity and wellbeing.

Personalized content discovery based on emotional state

Integrated Pomodoro Timer

Stay focused with our built-in timer that syncs with your chosen mood, suggesting appropriate break activities and maintaining flow.

Focused work sessions with mood-synced breaks

Personalized Content

Access curated playlists from Deezer, movie recommendations from TMDB, and engaging games from Itch.io - all filtered for your current state.

How It Works

  • Choose Your Mood - Select from Focus, Relax, Energize, Creative, or Melancholy
  • Start Your Session - Set up your Pomodoro timer and browse curated content
  • Stay in Flow - Work with focus music, take breaks with recommended activities
  • Switch as Needed - Seamlessly transition between mood hubs as your day evolves
