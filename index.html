What is FlowState?

FlowState is a mood-driven productivity platform that adapts to your emotional state, helping you work smarter and feel better throughout your day.

Mood-Based Discovery

Select your current emotional state and receive curated music, movies, and games tailored to enhance your productivity and wellbeing.

Integrated Pomodoro Timer

Stay focused with our built-in timer that syncs with your chosen mood, suggesting appropriate break activities and maintaining flow.

Personalized Content

Access curated playlists from Spotify, movie recommendations from TMDB, and engaging games from Itch.io - all filtered for your current state.

How It Works

  1. Choose Your Mood - Select from Focus, Relax, Energize, Creative, or Melancholy
  2. Start Your Session - Set up your Pomodoro timer and browse curated content
  3. Stay in Flow - Work with focus music, take breaks with recommended activities
  4. Switch as Needed - Seamlessly transition between mood hubs as your day evolves
