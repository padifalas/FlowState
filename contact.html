FlowState is a mood-driven productivity platform that adapts to your emotional state. It combines a Pomodoro timer with personalized content recommendations (music, movies, games) to help you work smarter and feel better throughout your day.

Currently in its prototype stage as part of a university capstone project, it will continue evolving into a fully realized productivity app.

Absolutely! Collaboration ideas, design feedback, and technical suggestions are always welcome. Whether you're interested in contributing code, design work, or have ideas for new features, feel free to reach out through the contact form or connect via LinkedIn or GitHub.

5 Mood Hubs: Focus, Relax, Energize, Creative, and Melancholy - each with curated content.

Integrated Pomodoro Timer: Customizable work and break durations that sync with your mood.

Content Discovery: Personalized music playlists (Deezer), movie recommendations (TMDB), and games - all filtered for your current state.

No Accounts Required: Browse recommendations freely, though you'll need platform accounts to access full content.
