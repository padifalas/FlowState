Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Pomodoro timer work?

Our Pomodoro timer follows the classic 25-minute work sessions followed by 5-minute breaks. After four sessions, you'll get a longer 15-minute break. The timer syncs with your chosen mood hub to suggest appropriate activities.

Can I customize my timer settings?

Yes! You can adjust work and break durations to fit your personal productivity style. Access the timer settings by clicking on the timer widget.

How are content recommendations personalized?

Content is curated based on your selected mood hub using our integrated APIs (Spotify, TMDB, Itch.io). Each hub filters content specifically for that emotional state and productivity need.

Do I need accounts with Spotify or other services?

While you can browse recommendations without accounts, you'll need respective platform accounts to play music on Spotify, watch content, or play games directly.

Is FlowState free to use?

Yes! FlowState is completely free. We provide curated content and productivity tools to help you achieve your best work without any cost.
